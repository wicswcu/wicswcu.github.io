Career Opportunities
WiCS is proud to offer a variety of opportunities for career advancement among the club's members.
Grace Hopper Celebration
WiCS is fortunate to be able to send several members to the Grace Hopper Celebration of Women in Computing every year! The event, inspired by the legacy of female computer scientist Admiral Grace Murray Hopper, is the world's largest gathering of women technologists. The conference features 30,000+ attendees, 425+ sessions, and 600+ speakers. Additionally, the conference provides students with great opportunities to network, gain professional visibility, and learn about new technologies.
Guest Speakers
WiCS frequently hosts guest speakers from a variety of different areas in the computer science industry. WiCS has had speakers from Microsoft, Vertex, and Vanguard. Speakers usually provide valuable details about their experiences in the field as well as tips for getting a job.
Volunteer Opportunities
WiCS is always looking for opportunities to work with citizens of the West Chester Communtiy.
The Melton Center
WiCS has had the incredible opportunity to work with the Charles A. Melton Arts and Education Center, bringing Lego Spike and Mindstorm Robots to give the students an introduction to computer science concepts in a relaxed, explorative atmosphere. The primary mission of the Melton Center "is to provide civic, arts, recreation, and education programs and services to underserved families in the West Chester area".
